Our Story & Journey

Celebrating the rich tradition of Tamil poetry by blending classical literature with modern technology.

🎯

Our Mission

Tamil Kavithai is a dedicated digital sanctuary created to preserve and celebrate Tamil literature's rich poetic traditions. We bridge the gap between ancient art forms and modern digital experiences, creating a space for reflection, learning, and artistic expression.

🚀

What We Do

We curate exceptional Tamil poetry, present it in highly readable typographic designs, and build creative tools. With our AI Assistant, users can collaborate with advanced generative models to write their own poems, making literary creation accessible to everyone.

🤝

Get Involved

This is a community-driven effort. If you are a poet, translator, or developer who wants to contribute submissions, enhance translation sets, or suggest features, feel free to reach out to us at apravint@gmail.com.

📈 The Journey

Key milestones in the evolution of our platform.

2023

Project Genesis 🚀

Launched the first web prototype of pravintamilan.com to share handpicked Tamil verses with clean layout configurations.

2024

Going Mobile 📱

Compiled the platform into a lightweight Android application using Capacitor, introducing local storage and offline capabilities.

2025

AI Integration 🤖

Integrated the Gemini API to introduce the "AI Poet" module, helping users generate and decode complex Tamil verses.

2026

Modern Redesign ✨

Rebuilt the system design tokens, added real-time search filters, bento widgets, and premium canvas editing tools.

Our Core Values

The fundamental principles that guide our development choices.

🏛️

Respect for Heritage

We carefully verify and honor original authors, translators, and historical contexts of all poetry displayed.

📖

Typographic Clarity

We prioritize readability with Outfit fonts and optimized line heights so poetry reads naturally on any display size.

🔒

Absolute Privacy

We do not use tracking cookies. Your bookmark choices and notes remain securely saved inside your local browser context.
